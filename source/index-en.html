Molytvoslov (prayer booklet) is your guide to communion with God.

Molytvoslov app contains internationally known prayers, such as The Lord’s Prayer and Psalm 91, as well as prayers for repentance, health and protection. You can access all of these prayers in five languages: Russian, Ukrainian, English, Romanian, and Armenian.

Download from the App Store Download from Google Play

Download Molytvoslov to make the known prayers your own.

For a deeper experience, the texts of the prayers are also available in the audio format.
